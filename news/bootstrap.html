ABOUT-US

Uganda Polymath Technical Institute

Uganda Polymath Technical Institute started in first june 2020 to provide uganda with a unique private institute of the higest quality.We develop students skills and capacity to effectively self market when job seeking, making our students highly demanded and employable.
UPTI is located in Kagoma Kawepe Central Region in waksio district.
UPTI is a thriving coummunity of scholars and students engaged in the pursuit of advanced knowledge, practical skills and human value.
We are a center for learning and service.The founder’s vision is to ensure that the students be fully prepared for the worlds enterprenuership and employment.
